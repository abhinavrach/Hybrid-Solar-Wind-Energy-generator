#  Hybrid Solar-Wind Energy Generator

A hybrid renewable energy system combining **solar panels** and a **Vertical Axis Wind Turbine (VAWT)** using a **Darrieus + Savonius** design for efficient power generation in roadside environments.

##  Concept

This system integrates two renewable sources:

-  **Solar Energy** – Generates power during daytime  
-  **Wind Energy (VAWT)** – Captures wind from vehicle movement  

### Why Hybrid?
- Works in **both sunny and windy conditions**
- Ensures **more consistent power output**
- Ideal for **urban roadside environments**

---

##  Wind Turbine Design

### Darrieus Rotor
- Lift-based turbine  
- High efficiency at moderate speeds  
-  Cannot self-start  

### Savonius Rotor
- Drag-based turbine  
-  Self-starting at low speeds  
- Lower efficiency  

###  Hybrid Advantage
Combining both:
- Self-start capability  
- Improved efficiency  
- Better performance in turbulent wind

##  Components Used

- Solar Panel  
- DC Motor / Generator  
- Darrieus + Savonius Blades  
- Rectifier Circuit  
- Battery / Capacitor (optional)  
- Supporting Frame  

---

##  Working Principle

1. Wind rotates the VAWT → generator produces electricity  
2. Solar panel generates DC power from sunlight  
3. Both sources contribute to output  
4. Power can be stored or used directly (e.g., LEDs)
